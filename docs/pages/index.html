Investor layer index for financial visibility, updates, KPI, and controlled shareholder-facing dashboards.

This route establishes the sovereign investor layer inside the website architecture. It is the future-facing environment for investor relations, performance reporting, financial transparency, KPI tracking, IPO readiness, and controlled access for shareholders and approved capital participants.

Layer boundary premise

The Investor layer remains separate from the public website, the internal Office layer, and the future ICOS product layer. It should evolve as its own sovereign environment with controlled access, capital-facing reporting, and investor-scoped dashboards.

Future access model

This layer is being prepared for future investor-specific authentication, approved visibility scopes, protected financial reporting, and role-aware capital dashboards without requiring structural re-architecture.

Governance direction

Investor-facing data should only be published from approved financial and governance pathways. The private vault remains the source of truth, while only approved investor outputs should be exported into this layer.
