Layer boundary premise

The Investor layer remains separate from the public website, the Office layer, and the future ICOS product layer. It should mature as a sovereign investor-facing environment for approved financial visibility, capital-facing reporting, and controlled shareholder dashboards.

Protected access direction

This layer is being structured for future protected investor authentication, controlled visibility scopes, shareholder-aware dashboards, and capital-facing financial access without requiring structural re-architecture.

Governance direction

Investor-facing financial information should only move into this layer through approved financial, legal, and governance pathways. The private vault remains the source of truth, while only approved investor outputs should be exported here.
